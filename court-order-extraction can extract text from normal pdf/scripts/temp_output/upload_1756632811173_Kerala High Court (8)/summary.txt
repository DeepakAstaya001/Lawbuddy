On 2 February 2024, Bail Application No. 41 of 2024 filed by Ibrahim, aged 36, accused in Crime No. 922/2023 of Vengara Police Station. He is charged under Section 286 IPC and Sections 4(b) & 5 of the Explosive Substances Act, 1908, for allegedly conducting illegal quarrying and blasting granite without a license, endangering life and property. Anticipatory bail was denied due to concerns about proper investigation and potential impact on further crimes. The court directed Ibrahim to surrender within two weeks, after which his bail request will be considered on merits. No fines or prison sentences were imposed in this decision.
