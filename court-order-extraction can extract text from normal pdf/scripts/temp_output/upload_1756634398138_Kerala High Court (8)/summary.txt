"On February 2, 2024, the High Court of Kerala at Ernakulam dismissed Bail Application No. 41/2024 filed by Ibrahim, aged 36, accused in Crime No. 922/2023 of Vengara Police Station. He was charged under Section 286 IPC and Sections 4(b) & 5 of the Explosive Substances Act, 1908, for allegedly conducting illegal quarrying and blasting granite without a license, endangering life and property. The court denied anticipatory bail but issued directions for Ibrahim to surrender within two weeks, after which his bail request could be considered on merits. If he fails to surrender, the Investigating Officer is free to arrest him."
