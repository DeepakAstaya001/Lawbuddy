On February 2, 2024, the High Court of Kerala at Ernakulam delivered an order in Bail Application No. 41/2024 filed by Ibrahim, aged 36, accused in Crime No. 922/2023 of Vengara Police Station. He is charged under Section 286 IPC and Sections 4(b) & 5 of the Explosive Substances Act, 1908 for allegedly conducting illegal quarrying and blasting granite without a license, endangering life and property. Anticipatory bail was denied due to concerns over proper investigation. Ibrahim is directed to surrender within two weeks, after which his bail request can be considered on merits. No fines or penalties were specified in the order.
